## üöÄ Getting Started

### First Launch

When you first open Modelo, you'll be guided through a quick setup process:

1. **Welcome Screen**: Introduction to Modelo's capabilities
2. **Profile Creation**: Set up your personal style profile
3. **Demo Data Option**: Choose to start with sample data or create from scratch

### Creating Your Profile

Your profile is the foundation for personalized recommendations. Complete these sections:

#### Basic Information
- **Name**: Your preferred name for personalization
- **Gender**: Helps with style recommendations
- **Age Range**: Influences style suggestions

#### Body Analysis
- **Body Type**: Choose from Pear, Apple, Hourglass, Rectangle, or Inverted Triangle
- **Measurements**: Optional but helpful for fit recommendations
- **Face Shape**: Oval, Round, Square, Heart, or Diamond

#### Style Preferences
- **Favorite Colors**: Select colors you love wearing
- **Disliked Patterns**: Patterns you prefer to avoid
- **Style Personality**: Classic, Trendy, Bohemian, Minimalist, etc.
- **Formality Preference**: Casual, Business Casual, Formal

#### Skin Analysis
- **Undertone**: Warm, Cool, or Neutral (affects color recommendations)
- **Seasonal Colors**: AI will suggest your best color palette

## üëî Managing Your Digital Wardrobe

### Adding Items

#### Manual Entry
1. Tap the **"+"** button in the wardrobe section
2. Fill in item details:
   - **Name**: Descriptive name (e.g., "Navy Blazer")
   - **Category**: Top, Bottom, Dress, Outerwear, Shoes, Accessory
   - **Color**: Choose from 13+ color options
   - **Pattern**: Solid, Stripes, Polka Dots, Floral, etc.
   - **Fabric**: Cotton, Silk, Wool, Denim, etc.
   - **Fit**: Fitted, Loose, Tailored, etc.
   - **Season**: Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter, All-Season
   - **Tags**: Custom tags for organization
3. Tap **"Save"** to add to your wardrobe

#### Photo Capture (Future Feature)
- Take photos of items for visual recognition
- AI automatically detects color, pattern, and type
- Edit AI suggestions before saving

### Organizing Your Wardrobe

#### Filtering Options
- **By Category**: View only tops, bottoms, dresses, etc.
- **By Color**: Filter by specific colors
- **By Season**: Show seasonal items
- **By Tags**: Use custom tags for organization

#### Sorting Options
- **Recently Added**: Newest items first
- **Most Worn**: Popular items first
- **By Rating**: Highest rated items first
- **Alphabetical**: Sort by name

### Item Management

#### Viewing Item Details
- Tap any item to see full details
- View wear history and statistics
- See AI-generated styling suggestions

#### Editing Items
1. Tap item to open details
2. Tap **"Edit"** button
3. Modify any information
4. Save changes

#### Rating System
- Rate items 1-5 stars based on how much you like them
- AI uses ratings to improve recommendations
- Higher-rated items appear more often in suggestions

#### Usage Tracking
- **Wear Count**: Automatically tracks how often you wear items
- **Last Worn**: Records when you last wore each item
- **Usage Patterns**: AI analyzes your wearing habits

## üé® AI Outfit Generator

### Generating Outfits

#### Basic Generation
1. Go to **"Outfits"** tab
2. Tap **"Generate New Outfits"**
3. Select **Occasion**:
   - Casual: Everyday, relaxed settings
   - Work: Professional, office environments
   - Formal: Special events, elegant occasions
   - Party: Social gatherings, celebrations
   - Date: Romantic outings
   - Workout: Exercise, athletic activities
4. Choose **Weather** (optional):
   - Hot, Warm, Mild, Cool, Cold
   - Rainy, Snowy
5. Tap **"Generate"** to get AI suggestions

#### Advanced Options
- **Style Preference**: Override default style for specific occasions
- **Color Focus**: Request outfits featuring specific colors
- **Avoid Items**: Exclude certain items from suggestions

### Understanding Recommendations

#### Outfit Cards
Each suggestion shows:
- **Visual Preview**: Color-coded representation of items
- **Item List**: Names of all pieces in the outfit
- **AI Score**: Compatibility rating (0-100%)
- **Occasion Tag**: Recommended use case

#### AI Insights
- **Why This Works**: Explanation of color harmony and style logic
- **Body Type Benefits**: How the outfit flatters your shape
- **Styling Tips**: Suggestions for accessories or modifications

### Managing Outfits

#### Saving Favorites
1. Tap the **heart icon** on any outfit suggestion
2. Add to your saved outfits collection
3. Access saved outfits anytime from the outfits tab

#### Rating Outfits
- Rate outfits 1-5 stars after wearing them
- AI learns from your ratings to improve future suggestions
- Higher-rated combinations influence similar recommendations

#### Outfit History
- View all previously generated outfits
- See which outfits you've worn and when
- Track outfit success rates

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "No Outfit Suggestions Generated"
- **Cause**: Insufficient wardrobe items or restrictive filters
- **Solution**: Add more items or broaden occasion/weather criteria

#### "AI Recommendations Don't Match My Style"
- **Cause**: Incomplete profile or insufficient feedback
- **Solution**: Update profile details and rate more outfits

#### "App Running Slowly"
- **Cause**: Large wardrobe database or device limitations
- **Solution**: Archive old items or restart the app
